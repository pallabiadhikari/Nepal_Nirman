About Nepal Nirman

Nepal Nirman is a citizen-driven platform that empowers individuals to report incidents, document injustices, and stay informed about civic issues. Our mission is to foster accountability and transparency by giving the public a voice and creating a secure space for evidence collection and reporting.

Why Nepal Nirman?

In many societies, civic issues such as corruption, discrimination, and injustice often go unreported due to fear or lack of access. Civic Hub aims to change that by providing a simple, safe, and secure platform where users can:

  • Submit incident reports anonymously
  • Attach evidence (photos, videos, documents)
  • Track civic issues and election updates
  • Access important contact numbers and legal rights

Our Vision

To create an informed, empowered, and engaged society that holds institutions accountable and ensures justice for all.
